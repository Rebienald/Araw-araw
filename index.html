Once Young, Once Lost, Now Forever Found

The song Araw-Araw is about loving someone every single day.
It’s about choosing them again and again, no matter what happens.
The song expresses how being with that person makes life better and more meaningful.

Even though we were apart for two years, your love never wavered, and it’s still the same. Thank you for

be my araw araw, the girl I will choose every single day, the girl I will love unconditionally, the girl who
